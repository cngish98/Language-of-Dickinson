Methods of Markup for the Emily Dickinson Elision Project

Selecting the Poems

To begin our mark up, we selected 90 poems from Emily Dickinson's corpus that span the early, middle,
and late periods of her career. We determined these periods by selecting spans of several years at a
time that we felt were significant time periods in her career. The early period spans from 1850-1862,
the middle period spans from 1863-1874, and the late period spans from 1875-1886. The link to the
database we used to gather our resources is included below

Emily Dickinson Database

Imagery Markup

In order to mark up the imagery we felt was significant in the poems we selected, Rachel began by
creating a Relax NG schema that included a myriad of adjectives and nouns we felt were worth marking
up. I wont include them all here, but certainly some of the most prevalent examples of imagery we
noticed had to do with nature, religion, and animals. Given are restrictive schema, we felt that by
marking up portions of text that made reference to things of that nature, we have gathered a cohesive
catalogue of imagery-based mark up.
