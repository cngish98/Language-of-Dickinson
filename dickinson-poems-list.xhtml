Potential Dickinson poems for markdown

Note: I selected the editions mostly based on which one I thought most obviously displayed a poem's elision.

The Johnson edition tended to have more annotations or notes on what would have been elided, so I chose those if I saw them.

Otherwise, I selected based on readability, which usually correlated with the Franklin edition.
